Welcome!

The Hough Orchestra is a group of talented musicians in grades 9-12 based out of William Amos Hough High School in Cornelius, North Carolina. Directed by Mr. Hugh Haddad, we have several concerts over the course of the year in the fall, winter, and spring and wish to bring our love of music to the surrounding community. Be sure to check back for any updates about upcoming performances, news about trips, and fundraising by the ensemble.
